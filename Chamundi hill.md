# Mysore chamundi hill
The Mysore hill is one of India’s most famous landmarks. It is known for its stunning architecture and rich history.  

## Visiting Hours  
- Monday–Sunday: 10 AM to 5:30 PM  
- Entry Fee: ₹50 (Adults), ₹30 (Children)
